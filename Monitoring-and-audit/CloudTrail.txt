CloudTrail
    - provides governance, compliance and audit for your AWS Account.
    - enabled by deafault
    - get an history of events/ API calls made within your aws account.
    - can put this logs to cloudwatch or s3.
    - Events:
        - Management events : by default trails configured to log this events, can seperate read and write events.
        - Data events: not logged because of high volume operations.S3 object level activity.
        - Cloud trail insights events:
            -  Enable CloudTrail insights to detect unusual sctivity in your acoount.
            - continuously analyze write events to detect unusual patterns.
            - work swith s3, Eventbridge further
    - Retention is 90 days by default.
    - to keep events longer than retention , log them to S3 and then use Athena to query them.
